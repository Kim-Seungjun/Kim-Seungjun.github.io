Bing Concert Hall

The Bing Concert Hall at Stanford will play a vital role on campus and in the local communtiy. The hall's exceptional acoustics and state-of-the-art technical capabilities will showcase the word's finest performers.

The hall is scheduled to open early in 2013. The concert hall will have 844 seats in an intimate vineyard-style configuration, with terraced sections wrapping wrapping all the way around the atage.
